Please check the Updated Agency Transfer List first before transferring the call to determine whether we transfer the call to Account Services or the Agency.

If the agency is listed on the "Agencies that Get All Calls" we can go ahead and transfer them to the provided telephone number.

"Before I transfer the call, is there anything else I can help you with?

If no:

"Please stay on the line while I transfer you.. Please hold.."

If the agency is listed on the "DO NOT Transfer List", please transfer the call to Account Services. 866-886-4808 / 866-205-6009.
Please note that the number is for internal use only and we don't provide them to the caller.

"To better assist you, I will be transferring the call to ____________, to help you find a coverage that would suit your needs / fit your budget.


"Before I transfer the call, is there anything else I can help you with?

If no:

"Please stay on the line while I transfer you.. Please hold.."

Reminders:
*We should always attempt a warm transfer!
 This allows us to share information directly with the representative who answers.
 This also allows us to verify that there isn't an issue with the call connecting.
 Include the name of the representative in your notes!

*When a warm transfer cannot be completed within 2 minutes, inform the caller that they will be transferred to the queue, then complete the transfer.
  Certain types of cold transfers will require an email follow up (billing & agency/retention email templates/guides have been provided for these cases).
  CSRs should have their notes completed when transferring so that the next representative can use them to assist the caller.
  Include the transfer details in your notes (Reason for transfer, left caller in Queue due to no connection in 2 minutes)!
