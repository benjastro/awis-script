MEMBER CLAIMS/NEEDS PORTAL FAQs

  • What is needed for a member to register?
    Member ID, Date of Birth, email address

  • What will the username be for member first time registration?
    Member ID

  • What will the password be for member first time registration?
    Date of Birth of primary member

  • What can the member use the portal for?
    Claims/Needs Submissions and Member Eligibility.

  • Can a member see plan benefits ex: copays and plan details?
    No, there are no plan details shown in the portal. Members can only see claims/needs submission and eligibility.
