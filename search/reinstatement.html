Reinstatement

If a member would like to reinstate or active their terminated account.

  • Check first when was the plan terminated. Please note that Cancellation Date/Termination date refers to the end of their coverage period while Date of Cancellation refers to the date it was requested or processed to be cancelled.
  • More than 30 days from Cancellation Date: We can no longer reinstate their account, offer to refer them to their agency for enrollment of a new plan
  • Less than 30 days from Cancellation Date: transfer to Billing
  • Also, advise the caller that it may be possible for them to be charged twice because they will be charged for the current/prior month plus the upcoming month.
