Legal Threat

If a caller threatens to sue us, call the police on us, report us to the insurance commission etc. we must send an email to Internalcomplaints@ahcminc.com

Please make sure to follow the correct legal threat template.
On the subject line:

In the section for (Your Department) please type "MS" or "Member Services" DO NOT TYPE "CSR". In "Type of legal threat:" Please describe the threat they made not just that it was a "legal threat".

moving forward we need all internal complaints when being submitted to include the (STATE) - (member id#) on the subject line for example this member resides in California so just need to put (department / CA / ID# and No Action/Action Needed) that is the only update on the template.

Action needed VS Action not needed:

ACTION NEEDED: There was no resolution at the end of the call, the member requires a call back to try to resolve the complaint and more investigation on the account is required.
NO ACTION NEEDED: Members' complaint was resolved, the member does not need to be called, and no further action needs to be made on the account.
