NO SALE STATE

  • DO NOT tell the member they are in a NO SALE STATE.

  • Use this verbiage: "Please allow me a moment to update your account."

  • Reach out to Account Services @ 866-886-4808 to verify if there is a plan in the member's state. If there is no plan in the member's state, please follow the No Sale State Process.
     o DO NOT discuss any refunds with the member.
     o DO NOT discuss any cancellation with the member.

  • If there is no answer from Account Services, send an email to outreach@ahcminc.com and copy your SUP/TL. Include the member's correct address. The response time from Outreach is 72 business hours.

  • If Account Services does have an available plan in the member's state of residency, connect the call to Account Services for further assistance.

  • If Account Services does not have an available plan in the member's state of residency, transfer the partner to Member Services Conversion Que extension 53110.

  • If we cannot offer the member a new plan where they live, Account Resolutions or Outreach will cancel the account with a refund due to the products not being offered in the partners state.
