Subrogation Process

Script

CSR: Thank you for calling Member Services, my name is “Name.” How may I assist you today?
Attorney: I am calling regarding my client John Doe, regarding a subrogation and to inquire on any claims that have been paid regarding their accident.

CSR: I would be more than happy to assist you with this request. May I please have the Member ID. Attorney: My client's member ID is M0000000.

We ask members and attorneys to send letter of representation to subbrogation@blackhawktpa.com.

If there are no notes on the account about us having letter or representation:

CSR: Thank you for providing the member ID. May I have the date of the accident? After reviewing the notes on file, I noticed that we have not received a letter of representation. For security purposes, we must have the letter on file to disclose account information. Would you like me to provide you with our E-mail? Subrogation@blackhawktpa.com

CSR: Once we have received the Letter of Representation, one of our representatives will contact you within 48-72 business hours and will be able to provide eligibility information. Our claims department will also be able to review any claims information related to the accident.

No notes are on file but the attorney states they previously sent Letter of Representation:

CSR: Can you please confirm the date of when the letter of representation was previously sent? I will send a follow up email to have the team contact you regarding your request.

Subrogation request and letters of representation are received by outreach and then posted on the account. Once the document is received and visible on the account this will represent HIPAA so you may assist with request they may be inquiring about.   • DO NOT release any information to the caller.
  • Check the NOTES tab for a Letter of Representation
