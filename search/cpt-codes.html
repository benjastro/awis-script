CPT Codes

CPT Codes - WHAT TO SAY AND WHY WE DON'T TAKE THEM!

  • Team if a provider gives you a CPT Code to verify if the service is covered, please state, "We don't interpret CPT codes but if you can specify what the service is, I can check if the benefits is supported under the member's plan."

  • The reason we don't interpret CPT codes is because we are NOT medically trained on medical terminology and medical codes. Don't put yourself in a compromising position trying to interpret CPT codes. Just ask the caller to once again specify what the service is for team!
